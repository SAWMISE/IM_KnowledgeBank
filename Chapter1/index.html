Core Definition

International business encompasses all cross-border exchanges of goods, services, or resources between nations. It goes beyond simple trade to include transfers of people, intellectual property, and contractual assets.

Entrepreneurship

Forms of International Business

Business Forms

Importing

Selling products and services sourced from other countries

Exporting

Selling domestic products and services in foreign markets

Foreign Direct Investment

Investing assets directly into foreign countries' infrastructure and operations

Government Involvement

Diplomatic Missions

Embassies and consulates facilitating international relations

International Treaties

Agreements like NAFTA, Kyoto Protocol governing cross-border activities

Supraorganizations

UN, WTO providing governance frameworks for global commerce

NGOs

Nonprofit Organizations

Addressing issues that transcend national boundaries

Advocacy Groups

Promoting human rights, environmental protection, and social justice

International Development

Supporting economic and social development across borders

The Globalization Debate

"Flat World" Perspective

Thomas Friedman

Markets are increasingly interconnected through technological "flatteners"

  • Internet and connectivity
  • Outsourcing capabilities
  • Supply-chain integration
  • Open-source collaboration
  • Information democratization
VS

"Multidomestic World"

Pankaj Ghemawat

Significant differences persist between countries - CAGE Framework:

Cultural

Language, norms, values

Administrative

Government policies, trading relationships

Geographic

Distance, time zones, climate

Economic

Income levels, costs, infrastructure

Ethics in International Business

Ethical standards vary across cultures and regions, creating unique challenges in international business contexts.

Ethical Decision-Making Framework

1

Identify the Issue

2

Gather Facts

3

Evaluate Alternatives

Consider multiple approaches using different ethical frameworks

4

Test the Decision

Assess whether you can comfortably explain your choice publicly

5

Implement & Learn

Act on your decision and review outcomes for future improvement
